How this list stays current
  • Automatic, daily: newsletter subscribers (from Ghost) and authors (from newly published pieces) refresh on their own.
  • Manual: donors (a Donorbox export) and any whole new list — hand the CSV to Claude Code to fold in.
  • Edits & manual adds here save automatically to the shared store; they show for you now and for everyone on the next daily refresh.
